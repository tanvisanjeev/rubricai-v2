AI Transparency
Responsible AI evaluation.
Built on six principles that ensure every evaluation is fair, auditable, and human-validated.
1
Evidence-Based Scoring
Every score backed by direct quotes from the transcript. No black-box grading.
2
Rubric-Governed
All evaluations grounded in your published rubric. AI matches evidence — never invents it.
3
Confidence Scoring
Every evaluation includes a confidence score. Below 60% is auto-flagged for review.
4
Instructor Override
Every AI score can be reviewed and overridden. AI assists — educators decide. Always.
5
Data Privacy
Student data processed locally. No external storage. Exports download to your machine.
6
Consistency & Fairness
Every student evaluated against the same rubric by the same model. Eliminates evaluator bias.
