AI-Powered Student Assessment

Upload interview transcripts and receive automated, rubric-aligned scores across Communication and Critical Thinking, consistently, at scale, and in a fraction of the time.

Responsible AI

Scores are grounded in a research-backed rubric, not black-box judgments. Every score includes a rationale and evidence from the transcript.

Transparent Scoring

Instructors can see exactly why a student received a score, supporting fair, explainable, and evidence-based assessment decisions.

Saves Hours of Work

What takes an instructor hours to evaluate manually is completed in minutes, freeing time for meaningful feedback and instruction.

Consistent Across Students

AI evaluation applies the same rubric criteria to every student equally, removing evaluator fatigue and subjective variation.

Supports Student Growth

Detailed per-indicator feedback gives students clear, actionable insight into their communication and critical thinking skills.

Scalable Across Cohorts

Evaluate one student or an entire class with the same effort. Designed to scale as programs grow without increasing instructor workload.
